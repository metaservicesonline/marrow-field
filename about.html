About Us

Built on ground we'd stand on ourselves.

Marrow & Field exists because too many people in this market have been sold land that didn't exist, or promises that evaporated after payment.

We started Marrow & Field after watching too many buyers get burned — land sold twice, titles that didn't hold up, agents who disappeared the moment money changed hands.

So we built the opposite. Every plot we list has been checked against state land registry records before it goes up. Every property has been inspected by someone on our team, in person, not just photographed.

We're not the biggest name in Rivers State real estate. We'd rather be the most trusted one.

Our Standard

How we vet every listing.

Title verification

Every plot is cross-checked against official land registry records before it's ever shown to a buyer.

Physical inspection

Someone from our team visits every property and plot in person — photos alone are never enough for us to list something.

Transparent pricing

The number on the listing is the number you pay. No surprise fees introduced after you've committed.
